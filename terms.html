2. Description of Service

PickleCall is an AI-assisted ball tracking application designed for recreational pickleball and tennis. It uses your device's camera and on-device machine learning to detect ball bounces and provide IN/OUT line call suggestions.

What PickleCall Does

  • Processes camera footage locally on your device to detect and track ball movement.
  • Provides visual and audio signals (green/red overlay with optional beep) indicating whether a ball appears to have landed IN or OUT.
  • Stores short replay clips locally on your device for review.
  • Supports single-camera (Solo), two-camera (Dual), and four-camera (Quad) setups via your local Wi-Fi network.

What PickleCall Does NOT Do

  • PickleCall is not a certified referee system. It is a recreational assistance tool.
  • PickleCall does not make official or binding calls for any organised tournament, league, or competitive event.
  • PickleCall does not guarantee accuracy equivalent to a human official or certified line-call system.
  • PickleCall does not replace the need for mutual agreement among players on line calls.

5. Accuracy Limitations

PickleCall's line-call accuracy depends on multiple factors. Accuracy is not guaranteed to meet any standard required for competitive play.

FactorImpact on Accuracy
Camera angle and positionSuboptimal angles reduce detection reliability, particularly near sidelines.
Lighting conditionsLow light, glare, or inconsistent lighting may reduce detection confidence.
Court calibration qualityInaccurate corner calibration produces incorrect court coordinate mapping.
Ball visibilityBalls partially obscured, blurred by speed, or blending with court markings may be missed.
Device hardwareOlder or lower-specification devices may process frames less reliably.
Camera frame rateLower frame rates increase the risk of the ball being missed between frames.
Camera-to-ball distanceGreater distances reduce pixel-level precision.
Weather and environmental conditionsWind, rain, shadows, and reflective surfaces may affect detection.

PickleCall may return DISPUTED or NO CALL when confidence is low. These states are intentional design choices — PickleCall prefers to abstain rather than make a potentially wrong call.
